About me.

Chloe Corrigan is a developer with an extensive background in art and design. Chloe is passionate about knowledge and is dedicated to helping lives big and small. Chloe has worked independently as well as with teams to create effective and conscious applications with a thoughtful UI/UX that make them pleasant to use. Along with Chloe's design capabilities, she has used popular languages and frameworks/apis (javascript, jquery, HTML5/CSS3, bootstrap, node.js, React, mySQL, MongoDB, Sequelize, Mongoose) to build applications. She graduated from The Coding BootCamp at UNC in May 2017. In her spare time, she is the founder of Southern Fowl Society, an organization dedicated to providing concrete resources and information to fowl enthusiast. She currently resides on her 3-acre farm-full-of-rescues in Moncure, NC.
