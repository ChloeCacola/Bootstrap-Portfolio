About me.

Chloe Corrigan is a developer with an extensive background in art and design. Chloe is passionate about knowledge and is dedicated to helping lives big and small. Having a strong capability to empathize, Chloe has worked independently as well as with teams to create effective and conscious applications with a thoughtful UI/UX. Along with Chloe's design capabilities, she has used popular languages and frameworks, including:

  • Javascript
  • jQuery
  • HTML5
  • C#
  • .NET
  • Selenium
  • Bootstrap
  • node.js
  • Angular 2+
  • React
  • mySQL
  • MongoDB
  • Sequelize
  • Mongoose

as well as knowledge of JSON and APIs to build applications.
Chloe graduated from The Coding BootCamp at UNC in May 2017. Chloe has a natural curiousity and interest in technology, so she is always expanding her skills and eager to learn.

In her spare time, she is the founder of Southern Fowl Society, an organization dedicated to providing concrete resources and information to fowl enthusiast. She currently resides on her 3-acre farm-full-of-rescues in Moncure, NC.
